About & Contact

Hello! is me, Alan Nevens, making photographs and websites in Edinburgh. In photography, I particularly like taking pictures of people and of books (which are like people but better and more rectangular). In website design, I like small-scale, carefully made sites with personality. I made this one you’re looking at right now and I was shortlisted for a Webby and won a Gold Lovie for this one.

I particularly like working with people in the arts and charity sectors.
